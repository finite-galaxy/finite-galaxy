Welcome to Finite Galaxy,
a free and open source 
space exploration game!
All files are located at:
https://github.com/finite-galaxy/

Finite Galaxy is derived
from Endless Sky, a game
created by Michael Zahniser;
its files are located at:
https://github.com/endless-sky/
